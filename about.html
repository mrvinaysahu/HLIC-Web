ABOUT SCHOOL

Director Of School : Er. Om Prakash (B.Tech, B.Ed)

Welcome to H.L.School a home built on decades of value imbued traditions.

As educators we understand that children learn more from what you are and what you do than what you say. We continue to make every effort to walk our talk and the best practices in the campus are aimed at cultural, social and ecological awareness. One cannot be ignorant about the evident challenges, provocations and inclinations the society faces today and ultimately our children are allured into being vulnerable rather than being resilient. Unconsciously to a certain extent educators and parents are raising children from a perspective of their world, which no longer exists and will very soon be disparate. Therefore, we make boundless efforts to equip them culturally, morally, ethically and for every individual to mature in physical, emotional and mental strength.


H.LSchool, is a highly reputed educational institution situated in the heart of Banda. From the last three decades, the school upholds its motto "Knowledge is Enlightenment" and is successfully providing good quality education with advance technologies to the children in Banda remote areas. H.L.School started its journey on a rented building with few children. At present, school holds a very significant role in the field of education,and has its own well constructed building, spacious and ventilated classrooms all because of the hard work of the management and the dedicated staff. In the field of charity, the school helps the poor and needy whole heatedly. Every year the school provides free education to hundreds of poor and needy students.
